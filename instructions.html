General Instructions
📋
Multiple Choice Questions
The test contains 25 MCQ questions. Each question has 4 options and only one correct answer.
⏱️
Timed Questions – 20 Seconds Each
Every question has a 20-second countdown. If time runs out, the question is automatically skipped and you move to the next one.
✅
Marking Scheme
Each correct answer earns +4 marks. There is no negative marking. Skipped questions score 0.
🔀
Navigation
You can click on the question number panel on the right to jump to any question directly. Change your answer any time before submitting.
🚫
Do Not Refresh
Do not refresh the browser or press the back button during the exam. Doing so may reset your progress.
🏫
Official Results
Your score is shown immediately after submission. Official results will be announced by the college separately.
